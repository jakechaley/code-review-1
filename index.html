About me

I graduated from Boise State University in 2018 with a degree in Communications. Since graduating and moving to Portland, I've been kind of wandering aimlessly from job to job without really knowing what I want for a career. When I met one of my best friends, Josh, in 2019 I had no interest in programming. To be frank, it was something that seemed so daunting and inaccessible. It seemed like something I would never think twice about since I'm not really a computer person. It wasn't until Josh noticed I was continually unhappy with my work that he mentioned maybe I would like programming. Well, it only took 3 years of him getting on my back about it for me to actually do it, and now that I've begun, I have an itch for it... I'm extremely excited to be venturing down the journey that is programming and I can't wait to see where it takes me.

When I'm not in class or working on a new project the place I want to be is on stage. I've played/written music for as long as I can remember and it's my absolute favorite thing. There's nothing quite like playing something you wrote at full volume and full energy. Other than music, here are some other things I love:
  • My dog, Indie (Really any dog honestly)
  • Seeing Movies
  • Relaxing with friends
  • Going to the river on sunny days
